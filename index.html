About the App

Study China is an independent reference guide for international students who want to pursue a degree at a Chinese university. Browse universities, compare scholarships, explore majors, estimate costs and plan your application timeline — all in English.

  • Search and filter universities by city, ranking and tuition
  • Step-by-step scholarship application guidance
  • Estimated budgets for tuition, housing and living costs
  • Works offline — all data stored on device
Disclaimer: Study China is an independent informational guide. We are not affiliated with any university, government agency or scholarship provider. Information such as tuition fees, scholarship amounts and admission requirements may change; we do not guarantee its accuracy or completeness, and we do not guarantee admission or scholarship outcomes. Always verify details with the official websites of universities and scholarship programs before applying.
